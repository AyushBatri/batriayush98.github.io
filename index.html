    What We Do

    SoSpheres is India's leading assessment and hiring platform. We run assessments to measure employability across all domains - from programming to plumbing. SoSpheres works with 600+ corporate clients to execute entry level and lateral assessments and has access to more than a million candidates through its platform. We run 2 million+ assessments each year in 350+ cities; thus, everyday helping our corporate clients reduce interview bandwidth using our central assessment platform, helping our institutional clients measure and improve employability and helping candidates move ahead on their career path. Our vision is to ensure that everyone in the country gets an equal opportunity to build a career. We are the visionaries of this space with a strong growth trajectory and an exciting firm to be with. SoSpheres is part of Aon plc (NYSE: AON)
